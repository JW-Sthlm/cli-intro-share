Recap

Foundation episode. The point: stop browsing lists of "ten AI use cases for sellers". Build your list from a five-minute interview with the agent. We ran the Use Case Finder live, walked through the five-question interview, and read the ranked output and start-here pick out loud.

One prompt, three doors. CLI in the terminal. Clawpilot as a desktop app. M365 Copilot Cowork in chat. Same shift, three scales of freedom. Most people in this room will end up in Clawpilot once it ships, and that's fine.

Operator move: two questions every Monday. What can I stop doing by hand. What can I now attempt that I couldn't before. The second question is the bigger one.

Otto debut Otto landed in the meeting chat for the first time. Version zero. Knows this episode, the use case finder, the season schedule, and a small FAQ. @mention Otto to wake it up. By episode six it should know everything we covered plus your unanswered questions.
Take-home this week

Run the Use Case Finder on yourself

Five minutes. Use whatever tool you have. Five honest answers. Pick your start-here.

Bring that start-here pick to E2 next Tuesday. We'll use what you bring as the brief for the live deck demo. If you don't bring one, you watch. If you do, you might get your deck built in front of the room.
