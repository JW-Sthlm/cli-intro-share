S1E6 · The recipe
04
The whole recipe

Three ingredients. That's an agent.

Strip away the jargon and an agent like Otto is three things. None of them is code you write. One of them is just the questions your team keeps asking.

  • 1A persona. One markdown file. How it talks, what it helps with, what it refuses to do.
  • 2Knowledge. Your stuff. The FAQ, the handbook, the things you explain twice a week. Markdown, not a database.
  • 3A place to live. A Teams chat, where your people already are. The agent shows up when you @mention it.
