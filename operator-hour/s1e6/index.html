The sidekick was an agent. Now it's yours.

All season, Otto answered your questions in the cohort chat. Today we open the hood. You see exactly how Otto is built, from persona to knowledge to deploy, then fork the repo and stand up your own agent for your team. The finale, and the on-ramp to Season 2.

What we'll cover
  1. Otto, with the hood up Every @mention this season hit a Copilot-built agent I made with the same tools you've used for six weeks. No secret there. Today we open it all the way up: persona, knowledge, deploy.
  2. Otto's three moving parts A persona, a knowledge base, and a deploy. The knowledge is just the recap pages and transcripts you already watched. There is nothing exotic under here.
  3. Fork it, live We take the repo and stand up a second agent in front of you. Its own name, its own knowledge, its own Teams chat. No engineer in the room.
  4. Point it at your world Swap Otto's course knowledge for whatever your team keeps re-explaining. Same shell, your content. The agent learns your room instead of mine.
  5. Hand it to the community The repo goes public. Season 2 isn't hosted by me. It's run by whoever forks this and teaches their own room.
The shift For six weeks Otto might have looked like a feature someone at Microsoft switched on. It's simpler than that. It's a repo a non-coder can fork in an afternoon. The agent that ran the season is the same one you'll run for your team.
