S1E5 · The recipe

The recipe

Real work in your voice.

Five steps, the same every time. The two that matter most: teaching the agent how you actually talk, and stripping the robot back out before anything ships.

  • 1Start from a real thing. A win, an update, a session. Not a blank page.
  • 2Teach it your voice. Built from writing you already have: sent emails, Teams messages. No blog required.
  • 3Draft, then make it yours. Eighty percent in seconds, then you do the twenty.
  • 4Strip the AI tells. A pass that catches the em dashes, the rule-of-three, the robot smell.
  • 5Make it a system. An engine that drafts your weekly updates, ready to edit.

S1E5 · Keep it honest
08
Yours, and honest

Three rules keep it real.

A post in your voice only works if it's actually yours and actually true. Three rules, no exceptions.

  • 1You read every word before it ships. The agent drafts. You publish. Never the other way round.
  • 2No invented facts. Numbers, names, outcomes are yours and real. The agent writes, it doesn't make things up for you.
  • 3Run the humanizer, every time. If a reader can tell a machine touched it, it failed. The pass is not optional.
