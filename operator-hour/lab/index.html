The Operator Lab

You have Copilot in five places now. Which one do you open for which job? Pick a real task, run it across more than one surface, and feel the difference yourself. No watching me demo. You do the work, you find your door.

This is an early prototype. One use case is fully built so you can feel the shape. The rest land as the Lab grows, and the surface boundaries will firm up as Microsoft ships and tells us more. Try the live one, then tell me what's off.
First, the compass

How much do you want to be in the room?

Forget the product names for a second. Every AI surface sits somewhere on one simple question: how much do you stay in the room while it works? That's the whole map.

1 · Ask You drive, you watch You ask, it answers, you're there the whole time. Quick help while you work. M365 Copilot, GitHub Copilot App
2 · Hand off It drives, you check You brief it once, walk away, come back to work done. Multi-step jobs. CoWork, Copilot CLI
3 · Leave it running It watches and acts It runs without you asking. Recurring triggers and monitoring. Scout

As you move down, you get more power and pay a little more setup. Ask is open already. Leave it running takes more to wire up. That's the trade.

The honest part: they overlap

Most jobs fit more than one door

Clean buckets would be a lie. A lot of work can be done in two or three surfaces, just differently. The skill isn't memorizing a grid. It's feeling which door fits the size of the job in front of you.

Prep for a meeting sits right in the middle. You can Ask it, hand it off, or one day leave it running. The Lab lets you try each.

The five surfaces

Same idea, different settings

Microsoft ships the same agent idea at different openness settings on purpose. Some are locked and safe, some are open and powerful. Here's the quick read.

Ask

M365 Copilot

Inside Word, Teams, Outlook, Excel. Reach for it for fast jobs in an app you already have open. Zero setup.

Ask · Hand off

GitHub Copilot App

The same kind of help on desktop and your phone. Good for repo-adjacent work and picking things up on the go.

Hand off

CoWork

Hand off a multi-step job without a terminal, inside the Copilot you already have. The buttoned-up version of the CLI.

Hand off · open

Copilot CLI

Build your own skills, run deep multi-tool jobs, work overnight. The open power end. A terminal and some setup.

Optional module Leave it running

Scout

Agents that watch and act unasked. Enterprise-locked and safe. Needs access, so partners without it can skip this track.

Where Scout comes from. Scout is OpenClaw, the open-source agent project, made enterprise-ready. Same engine, guardrails on. You don't pick OpenClaw as a surface, it's the base underneath.
