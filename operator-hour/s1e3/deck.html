S1E3 · The engine
08
What it actually does

Three rules. That's the engine.

No clever AI deciding what matters to you. Three plain rules you can read in one breath. Dumb on purpose, so it never hides a real email.

  • 1Addressed to you stays put. Anything sent directly to you stays in the inbox. Always.
  • 2Noise gets its own room. Newsletters, notifications and broadcasts move to their own folders.
  • 3When unsure, keep it. If it can't tell, it leaves it in the inbox. It never guesses a real one into a folder.

And when it isn't sure, it does the safe thing and leaves it in front of you. You can read the whole logic in one breath. That's the point.
